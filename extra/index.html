CS 198-175: Extra Topics on the Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs

Information

Zoom link for extra lectures: go.cs61a.org/extra-lecture.

Recordings of the extra lecture Zoom feeds appear in the CS 61A bCourses Media Gallery.

These extra lectures are completely optional and open to everyone enrolled in 61A. They are held Thursdays 9-10am in 20 Social Sciences. 61A students can also enroll for 1 unit of P/NP credit in CS 198-175, Extra Topics on the Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs, using CCN 34111. To pass this course, you need to complete all three of the extra homework assignments.
